About Us

Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology is an integral part of Rajalakshmi Institutions, which is a name synonymous with student empowerment through excellence in higher education. Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology, started in 2008, is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and has NBA accredited courses.

Graduate Programmes offered:

  • B.E. Computer & Communication Engineering
  • B.E. Computer Science & Engineering
  • B.E. Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • B.E. Mechanical Engineering
  • B.Tech. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Academic Excellence

Departments

The strength and uniqueness of the college stems from its dedicated and committed faculty. With an unparalleled and genuine concern for students they provide great inspiration and motivation to the students.

Infrastructure

The academic buildings feature fully networked classrooms that will allow professors and students to use the latest instructional technologies as part of the learning process. The design is flexible enough so that new technologies can be incorporated as they develop.

Facilities

RIT has been consistent in maintaining excellent academic standards. Learning is an enjoyable experience here, with experienced faculty, inspiring libraries and stimulating discussion rooms, large playgrounds and state-of-the-art labs.

Placement

Training and Placement Cell is an integral part of the institute. The institute has provided complete infrastructure for effective functioning of the cell. Our students have delighting successes to their credit & today our alumni are spread across the world creating wonderful solutions working for the best organizations. Training activities are organized throughout the year in an effort towards preparing the prospective students for the campus selection programmes.
